Decorative Potions adds some placeable decorative potion tiles!

Each potion and flask has a decorative version, crafted using one potion at a heavy workbench

If you want potions, flasks, and alcohols from Calamity, check out WitherTaco's [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3174001921]Calamity Decorative Potions[/url] mod
